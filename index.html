Welcome to Wimbledon
Social Club


Founded in 1870 when the nearby railway line was built.


Situated close to Purley town centre, a CIU affiliated members club with
full social facilities including 4 Dartboards, 2 Full size professional standard
Snooker tables, Pool table, Skittles lane & a small stage for bands or small
events.

We have a number of teams in local leagues including darts and snooker.


We pride ourselves on a friendly and welcoming atmosphere,
we run a number of event nights during the year including quizzes, race nights and
live entertainment.

New members are always welcome Please call into the club or drop us a line
on our contact form on the website.
